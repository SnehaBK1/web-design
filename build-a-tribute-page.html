Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug in his later years.
Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist who is considered one of the greatest humanitarians of the 20th century. His work in developing high-yielding varieties of wheat and his efforts in the Green Revolution helped save millions from famine.
